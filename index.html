Synopsis

After her sister leaves home, Tari fights to protect her mother from her father’s abuse. Struggling to find hope, she joins a support group led by Nina, where she meets Baskara — a kind and patient man who helps her rediscover her strength. With the guidance of Pras, a compassionate uncle who understands her pain, Tari learns that healing begins when one dares to face the truth and embrace vulnerability. Bolehkah Sekali Saja Ku Menangis is a poignant story about courage, forgiveness, and the power of empathy.
